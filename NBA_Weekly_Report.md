## 📈 Methodology

### Power Rankings
- **Win Percentage:** 35%
- **Net Rating:** 30%
- **Offensive Efficiency:** 20%
- **Defensive Efficiency:** 15%

### Matchup Scores
- **Quality Score:** Average power rating of both teams (0-100)
  - Higher scores indicate stronger teams overall
  - Typical range: 30-70 based on current team strengths

- **Competitive Score:** How evenly matched teams are (0-100)
  - Calculated using exponential decay: 100 × e^(-difference/15)
  - Perfect matchup (0 point difference) = 100
  - 10 point difference ≈ 51 (close game)
  - 20 point difference ≈ 26 (moderate mismatch)
  - 30+ point difference ≈ <15 (blowout likely)

- **Overall Matchup Score:** 60% quality + 40% competitiveness
  - Balances team strength with game competitiveness
  - Best games: strong teams that are evenly matched

### Watchability Tiers
- 🔥 **MUST WATCH** (70+): Elite matchups between top teams
- ⭐ **HIGHLY RECOMMENDED** (60-69): High-quality, competitive games
- 👍 **WORTH WATCHING** (50-59): Solid matchups
- 📺 **Optional** (<50): Lower-tier or lopsided games
